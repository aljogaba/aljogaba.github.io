Research

My research focuses on the molecular epidemiology of viral diseases affecting swine production systems. I am particularly interested in the genetic diversity, evolution, and transmission dynamics of Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome Virus (PRRSV) and Porcine Circovirus type 2 (PCV2).

My work integrates molecular virology, epidemiological analysis, and field data from commercial swine farms to better understand pathogen circulation and support disease control strategies.
