Portfolio

Projects, collaborations, and highlights:

  • Genetic variability of PRRS virus in swine production systems in Jalisco and its relationship with clinical presentation ("Caracterización de la variabilidad genética del virus de PRRS en los sistemas de producción porcícola de Jalisco y relación con la presentación clínica") (2023–2024)

    Role: Co-investigator
    Institution: INIFAP
    Collaboration: Unión Regional de Porcicultores de Jalisco (URPJ)
    Funding: IPVS México Association
    Description: Molecular epidemiology study of PRRSV circulating in swine production systems in Jalisco
    Outputs: scientific publication
  • Development of diagnostic tests and immunogens for swine influenza virus based on its epidemiology in Jalisco ("Desarrollo de pruebas diagnósticas e inmunógenos del virus de influenza porcina basados en su epidemiología en el Estado de Jalisco") (2023–2025)

    Role: Co-investigator
    Institution: INIFAP
    Collaboration: Unión Regional de Porcicultores de Jalisco (URPJ)
    Funding: INIFAP
    Description: Development of immunogens and diagnostic tools for swine influenza virus based on epidemiological data from swine production systems in Jalisco
    Outputs: conference publications
  • Technology transfer for the management and utilization of swine production residues ("Transferencia de tecnología para el manejo y aprovechamiento de residuos porcícolas") (2022)

    Role: Project Leader
    Institution: INIFAP
    Collaboration: Unión Regional de Porcicultores de Jalisco (URPJ)
    Funding: Secretaría de Desarrollo Rural, Jalisco (SADER)
    Description: Training programs for producers focused on the management and utilization of agricultural residues with emphasis on swine production systems
    Outputs: training workshops and technical courses
  • Protocol for strengthening maize and pork production for food sovereignty in rural and indigenous communities of the Manantlán Biosphere Reserve ("Protocolo para el fortalecimiento en la producción de maíz y carne de cerdo para la soberanía alimentaria de las comunidades rurales e indígenas de la Reserva de Manantlán") (2021)

    Role: Project Leader
    Institution: INIFAP
    Collaboration: Unión Regional de Porcicultores de Jalisco (URPJ); Centro Universitario de la Costa Sur, Universidad de Guadalajara; Comisión Nacional de Áreas Naturales Protegidas (CONANP)
    Funding: CONACYT
    Description: Multidisciplinary initiative aimed at strengthening self-sufficiency in maize and pork production in rural and indigenous communities
    Outputs: project framework, strategic agenda, and outreach publications
  • Building the capacity of livestock practitioners in innovative on-farm production practices to mitigate the negative impact of climate change (2021)

    Role: Project Leader
    Institution: INIFAP
    Collaboration: Rural Agricultural Development Authority (RADA), Kingston
    Funding: Secretaría de Relaciones Exteriores – AMEXCID
    Description: Integration of innovative technologies and management practices to promote sustainability and mitigate the impacts of climate change in livestock production systems
    Outputs: professional training and international knowledge exchange
