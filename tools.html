Professional ecosystem

Swine Systems Laboratory

A professional ecosystem of web-based tools for swine production systems, epidemiological interpretation, molecular surveillance, and technical decision support. The tools are designed from field experience, research workflows, and applied epidemiology.

Available tools

Beta

SIPRO-Porcino

Integrated simulator for swine production systems. It projects productive flow, inventories, feeding costs, operating costs, replacement dynamics, economic scenarios, and decision indicators.

Open tool
Beta

Epi 2×2

Epidemiological calculator for 2×2 contingency tables. It estimates RR, OR, attributable risk, Pearson χ², confidence intervals, graphical visualization, and automatic interpretation.

Open tool
Platform

DiseasesMapMx

Genomic surveillance and epidemiological data visualization platform for PRRSV and PCV2 sequences in Mexican swine populations.

Open platform
In development

Future technical tools

Additional calculators and visualization modules for epidemiology, production analysis, biosecurity, surveillance, and applied decision-making.

Development principles

  • Applied epidemiology and swine production systems as the central framework.
  • Transparent formulas, traceability, and technical documentation.
  • Tools designed for professional use, teaching, and research communication.
  • Authorship, citation, and responsible reuse of technical material.
