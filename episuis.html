EPISUIS

Epidemiology-Driven Solutions for Swine Production Systems

Overview

EPISUIS is an independent initiative focused on improving swine production systems through the application of epidemiological principles. It integrates health, production, and data analysis to support informed decision-making and long-term system optimization.

The Problem

Conventional advisory approaches in swine production are often reactive, addressing clinical issues as they arise without fully understanding the underlying system dynamics. This leads to temporary solutions rather than sustained improvements.

Our Approach

Each farm is treated as a unique epidemiological system. EPISUIS analyzes disease dynamics as interactions between pathogens, hosts, environment, and management practices, enabling deeper understanding and targeted intervention.

MAEPP Framework

  • Epidemiological assessment at farm or territorial level
  • Identification of risk factors
  • Molecular characterization of pathogens
  • Analytical interpretation of data
  • Evaluation of impact on production parameters

What Makes EPISUIS Different

EPISUIS focuses on generating system-specific knowledge through integrated and longitudinal analysis. The objective is not only to solve current issues, but to anticipate future risks and enable sustainable improvements.

Outcomes

  • Improved production efficiency
  • Evidence-based decision-making
  • Better control of endemic and emerging diseases
  • Optimization of available resources
